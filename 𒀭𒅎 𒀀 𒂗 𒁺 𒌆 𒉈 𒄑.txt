In summary, the politics, literature, and historiography of Ancient Egypt are deeply intertwined. Texts like the "Instruction of Amenemhet I" and the "Instruction for Merikare" showcase the sophisticated ways in which the ancient Egyptians utilized literary forms for political ends.
